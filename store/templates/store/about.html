About

Who are we and what is this website about?
We are CS students learning web development looking to create
useful websites that others may benefit from. We created this website
with students' needs in mind. We realized that many students are in need
of selling their second hand item for one reason or another.

Perhaps you only moved to Glasgow temporarily for your studies and are now moving back?
Maybe you are just looking to sell items you no longer need in your room?
Whatever your individual circumstance is, this is the site for you!
